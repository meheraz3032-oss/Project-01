Welcome to Cumilla Polytechnic Institute

Comilla Polytechnic Institute is one of the leading government polytechnic institutes in Bangladesh. Located in Kotbari, Comilla, we are dedicated to producing highly skilled diploma engineers ready to meet the challenges of the 21st century.

We offer a vibrant academic environment with state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty, and a focus on practical, hands-on learning.
